Bio

Hello there! To be honest, I mainly made this site to experiment with Github pages and I thought the best way to do that was with a personal website. If you're interested, you can find a small section about me below.

Currently, I have three main passions that I work on while I search for a job. I love growing and cultivating plants, working on my restaurant app, and reading books (you can find a list of my top 5 favorite books below).

Horticulture

As of now, I am raising a variety of plants, mostly herbs and one small avocado tree, my prized gem. My love for horticulture probably stems from this ideology I have where I believe that with enough daily effort, anyone can grow and accomplish anything. I find this to be analogous to the way that plants grow in that day to day, there might not be that much change; however, weeks, months, or years of daily, incremental growth adds up and brings you closer to your goal.

Yuzu Menu

Yuzu Menu is the name of an app that I am currently developing that combines the restaurant review aspect of Yelp and the social network of Instagram. My first prototype was a React app with the entire backend utilizing Firebase. I soon realized that Firebase could barely cover the scope of what I wanted to achieve so I decided to write my own backend. My current stack includes an Express server, MongoDB for data storage, Redis for caching, S3 for file storage, EC2 for hosting, GraphQL for backend APIs, and React for a web frontend. Before I deploy my product, I plan to use Docker to containerize my app and NGINX to handle the load balancing. Keep your eyes peeled for the release of Yuzu v2!
